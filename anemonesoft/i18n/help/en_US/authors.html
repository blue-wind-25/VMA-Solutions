About the Authors

Aloysius Indrayanto

Aloysius Indrayanto got his M.Sc. in Computer Sciences in 2009 at Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM), Penang, Malaysia. He got his B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering in 2004 at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember, Surabaya, Indonesia.

At the present time, A. Indrayanto is a florist and technopreneur.

He established AnemoneSoft.com on early 2010. He co-established Fleur~de~Lis Florist Sby on early 2013. In early 2015, AnemoneSoft.com ceased providing services to 3rd party/client; it became an internal part of Fleur~de~Lis Florist. In middle 2021, AnemoneSoft.com is renamed to VMA Consultant..

A. Indrayanto is also an open source developer. He is the author of eCxx (https://sourceforge.net/projects/ecxx), a C++ library for AVR and NodeMCU tailored for micro LED displays and lighting effects. He is also contributing in the development of Platinum (https://sourceforge.net/projects/pt-framework), a comprehensive C++ framework that allows developers to write high-performance applications for many platforms with only one codebase.

A. Indrayanto worked as cluster administrator and research officer in Grid Computing Lab, Universiti Sains Malaysia during his M.Sc. study. He worked as software engineer in L4M GmBH, Germany via internet for one year (February 2005 to February 2006).

His main interest includes floral arrangement and decoration, microcontrollers, LED lighting, open source software, Linux, C/C++, Python, Java, HTML, and JavaScript.

He can be contacted via aloysius.indrayanto@gmail.com.

Gunawan Indrayanto

G. Indrayanto got his Dr. rer. nat (Magna Cumlaude) in 1983 at the University of Tuebingen, Tuebingen, Germany, under supervision of Prof. Dr. E. Reinhard and Prof. Dr. Dr. W.Voelter. He ever worked as visiting Researcher at Leiden University (1989), Duesseldorf University (1994 and 2002).

Presently G. Indrayanto is Professor at the Faculty of Pharmacy, Surabaya University, Surabaya, Indonesia. G. Indrayanto is former senior Professor (IVE) at Faculty of Pharmacy, Airlangga University, He was former member of the Indonesian National Board of Education (Badan Standard Nasional Pendidikan; 2009-2014), and former member of Research Council of Indonesian Higher Education Board (Dewan Pendidikan Tinggi; 2009-2011). In 2008 he was nominated as Leading Scientist by COMSTECH (OIC Standing Committee on Scientific & Technological Cooperation), and in 2009 he also was nominated as Excellent Researcher by SEA-EU-NET. He was awarded Achmad Bakrie Award 12 for Health Sciences (2014), recently he was honored Satya Lencana Karya Satya XXX (2017).

He served as editor- or advisory-board of some accredited Indonesian National Journals (Makara Journal of Sciences, Alchemy, J. Penelitian Kimia, Indonesian J. of Veterinary Sciences); he is also member of the Advisory Board of Journal Planar Chromatography (Springer, Germany), Former editor or contributing Editor of some volumes of the Serial book of Profile of Dug Substances, Excipients and Related Methodology (Academic Press-Elsevier, The Netherlands/USA), Editor of Advances in Pharmacological and Pharmaceutical Sciences (Hindawi, India), Guest editor for Frontiers in Pharmacology. He is served as invited reviewers of some International Journals (J. Pharm. Biomed. Anal, Talanta, J. Planar Chromatogr. Modern TLC, Journal Traditional and Complementary Medicine etc), and some National Journals.

G. Indrayanto currently has ca. 120 international publications consisted of original articles, chapters, short communications, and review articles. His publications, H-index, H-10 index were listed in Scopus, Research Gate, Google Scholar, Sinta and some other data bases.

Presently his main works are analytical method development (chemical and bioassay) and its validation, metabolite profiling, and natural product chemistry.

He can be contacted via gunawanindrayanto@yahoo.com.
